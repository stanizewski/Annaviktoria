ABOUT ME

With the aim of spreading love and healing through the expression of yoga, Anna believes that yoga is for everyone regardless of body type, background or physical experience. Anna started practicing more than 10 years ago and went through a blessing of a burn-out which opened her eyes to the holistic aspect of how yoga is both movement medicine and soul therapy. Today she is teaching courses, workshops and studio classes in Stockholm as well as our yoga retreats in Bali and Brazil. Her classes are infused with warmth, playfulness and a depth that inspires you to connect beyond the surface.
